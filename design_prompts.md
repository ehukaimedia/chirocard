# ChiroCard UI Design Prompts

Use these prompts with Gemini (Banana Pro) to generate the UI mockups. For best results, upload your logo (`android-chrome-512x512.png`) as a reference image if the tool supports it.

## 1. Dark Mode: "Bioluminescent Nature"

**Prompt:**
> A photorealistic, high-fidelity mobile app UI mockup for 'ChiroCard' in a premium 'Bioluminescent Nature' dark mode.
>
> **Style:** Cyber-organic, deep dark mode with glowing biological accents.
> **Background:** Deep Zinc-900 (near black) texture with subtle, faint green bioluminescent plant veins or fern silhouettes in the background.
> **Header:** 'ChiroCard' title in crisp white sans-serif font. Next to it, place a stylized green logo representing 'Lomi Lomi Hands'.
> **Main Content:** A 'Body Region Selector' interface.
> *   **Cards:** Floating, dark frosted glass cards (glassmorphism) with soft rounded corners.
> *   **Buttons:** Pill-shaped buttons for 'Neck', 'Shoulders', 'Upper Back', 'Lower Back'.
> *   **Status Indicators:** The 'Lower Back' button has a soft, organic Red pulse/glow (indicating an issue). The 'Neck' button has a soft Blue pulse/glow (indicating watch status).
> **Lighting:** Cinematic studio lighting, emphasizing the neon green rim lights and the glass textures.
> **Colors:** Primary Emerald Green (#177D4F) for branding, Leaf Green (#8AAB35) for accents.

## 2. Light Mode: "Morning Garden"

**Prompt:**
> A photorealistic, high-fidelity mobile app UI mockup for 'ChiroCard' in a fresh 'Morning Garden' light mode.
>
> **Style:** Clean, airy, holistic wellness aesthetic.
> **Background:** Crisp white or very soft off-white (#F8FAF9) with subtle, fresh green leaf textures or shadows of leaves.
> **Header:** 'ChiroCard' title in dark grey. Next to it, place a stylized green logo representing 'Lomi Lomi Hands'.
> **Main Content:** A 'Body Region Selector' interface.
> *   **Cards:** Clean white cards with very soft, diffused shadows (neumorphism-inspired but flatter).
> *   **Buttons:** Pill-shaped buttons with thin, elegant green borders.
> *   **Status Indicators:** The 'Lower Back' button has a soft pastel Red fill. The 'Neck' button has a soft pastel Blue fill.
> **Lighting:** Bright, natural daylight, soft shadows like morning sun through a window.
> **Colors:** Primary Emerald Green (#177D4F) for text/icons, fresh Leaf Green (#8AAB35) for highlights.
